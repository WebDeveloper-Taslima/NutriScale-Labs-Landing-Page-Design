About NutriScale Labs

At NutriScale Labs, we believe manufacturing is only the beginning. Based in the heart of Miami, Florida, we are more than just a supplement factory — we are your brand scaling partner.

With our state-of-the-art facility in Hialeah (2630 W 79th St, Hialeah, FL 33016), we produce high-quality pills, capsules, powders, and even pet supplements — all Made in the USA and backed by FDA-approved standards and full certifications.

But what truly sets us apart is our commitment to helping you grow your dream brand. From custom formulations and premium raw ingredients to label and logo creation, Shopify integration, and 1-on-1 consultation, we guide you every step of the way.

We are the only manufacturer that combines world-class production with real brand-building expertise. With NutriScale Labs, you don’t just get products — you get a partner who’s dedicated to scaling your business.
